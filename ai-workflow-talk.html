The Case for AI

Why AI for developers?

Not about replacing engineers — About removing friction from the work you already do every day
Save hours on repetitive manual tasks — The boring stuff gets done faster, or disappears entirely

Tool 05 / 07

Play Store Build Uploader

The Problem
Uploading builds to the Google Play Store required many manual steps and was prone to human error.
The Solution
A Python script that fully automates the AAB upload to Google Play Store production track — end to end.
What It Does
Automates upload of Android App to Google Play Store
Sets up Python environment and installs required dependencies
Authenticates securely using a Google service account
Uploads AAB and assigns it to the production track at 100% rollout
Copies release notes from the previous production release automatically
Validates, commits, and logs the release process for traceability
Consistent, hands-free release process every time

Your Turn

How to get started

1
Identify one repetitive task you do every week
Something that takes 10+ minutes and feels mindless — that's your target
2
Ask Copilot: "Help me automate this"
Describe the task in plain language. You don't need to know the solution upfront
3
Iterate — it's okay if the first version is rough
Ship something that works, then refine it. Don't wait for perfect before sharing
4
Share it with your team
Others almost certainly have the same pain. One tool can save everyone time
